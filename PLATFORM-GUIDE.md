# PepLab — User Guide

PepLab lets the peptide community pool money to pay for independent lab testing of vendor products. You chip in a few dollars, a sample gets tested, and the results go up for everyone to see.

---

## What Is This?

You buy peptides from a vendor. You have no idea if what's in the vial matches the label. PepLab fixes that.

Someone creates a **campaign** — they pick a peptide, a vendor, and a lab, then ask the community to fund the testing cost. If enough people contribute, the sample gets sent to the lab and the Certificate of Analysis (CoA) is published publicly. You benefit whether you contributed or not.

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Create an Account

Sign up with your email. Verify your email — you'll need it verified before you can contribute or withdraw.

### 2. Add Funds to Your Wallet

Go to **Wallet → Deposit**. You'll see your personal Solana deposit address with a QR code.

Send any of these stablecoins to that address:

- **USDT** — preferred, credited directly
- **USDC** — accepted, small conversion fee applies
- **PYUSD** — accepted, small conversion fee applies

> **Solana network only.** Do not send from Ethereum, BSC, or any other chain. Those funds cannot be recovered.

Your balance typically updates within a minute or two after your transaction confirms.

### 3. You're Ready

With a balance on the platform you can:

- Contribute to any open campaign
- Create your own campaign (requires at least $1.00 balance)
- Withdraw your balance back to any Solana wallet

## Contributing to a Campaign

Browse the home feed and tap any campaign that interests you.

The campaign page shows:

- What peptide is being tested and which vendor it came from
- Which lab will analyze it
- What tests are requested (purity, mass, sterility, endotoxins, identity)
- How much has been raised and what the goal is
- The fundraising deadline

Tap **Contribute**, enter an amount (or pick a quick amount), and confirm. The funds move instantly from your wallet to the campaign escrow. You'll get notifications as the campaign progresses.

**A few rules:**

- You cannot contribute to your own campaign
- Campaigns flagged for review are temporarily paused
- Contributions are non-withdrawable once made — if the campaign fails or misses deadlines, you get an automatic refund

## Creating a Campaign

You need a small minimum balance in your wallet to create a campaign (the platform sets this amount — check the app for the current requirement).

### What You'll Set Up

**The Goal**

- A title and description explaining what you're testing and why
- How much funding you need (USD)
- A funding threshold — for example, 80% means you'll proceed as long as at least 80% of the goal is raised
- A fundraising deadline

**The Samples**
For each physical vial you plan to send:

- The vendor you bought it from
- The peptide name
- When you bought it and any physical notes about the vial
- Which lab you're targeting

**The Tests**
Pick which tests the lab should run on each sample. Common options:

- **Purity** — what percent of the compound is actually the target peptide
- **Mass** — confirms the labeled amount is accurate
- **Identity** — confirms it's the right compound at all
- **Sterility** — pass/fail safety check
- **Endotoxins** — bacterial contamination check

The platform calculates your estimated lab cost as you go and helps you set a realistic funding goal.

## What Happens After Your Campaign Goes Live

### Fundraising

The campaign is open. Anyone with a balance can contribute. If you hit your funding threshold before the deadline, the campaign automatically marks itself as funded.

### You Lock It

Once funded, you confirm you're ready to proceed. This locks contributions (no more can be added) and starts a **7-day clock** for you to ship the samples.

### You Ship

You mail the physical samples to the lab. Mark them as shipped in the app. The lab now has **21 days** to complete testing.

### Results Come In

The lab sends back a Certificate of Analysis. You upload it to the platform as a PDF. The platform verifies it's a genuine result for your campaign using a unique code embedded in the document.

### Campaign Resolves

Once results are verified and published, the campaign resolves:

- You receive the escrow funds (minus the 5% platform fee)
- All contributors are notified and can read the published results

### If Something Goes Wrong

If deadlines are missed or the campaign doesn't reach its threshold, all contributors are refunded automatically. No action needed.

## Withdrawing Your Balance

Go to **Wallet → Withdraw**. Enter a Solana wallet address and the amount. Withdrawals go out as USDT.

---

## Notifications

You'll be notified when:

- Your deposit arrives
- A campaign you contributed to gets funded, ships samples, publishes results, or gets refunded
- Your withdrawal is sent
- Your campaign hits a milestone

Manage email preferences under **Account Settings**.

## Fees

| Action                     | Cost                              |
| -------------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| Depositing USDT            | Free                              |
| Depositing USDC or PYUSD   | Small conversion fee (0.5%)       |
| Contributing to a campaign | Free                              |
| Withdrawing                | Free                              |
| Successful campaign payout | 5% platform fee taken from escrow |
| Refund if campaign fails   | Free — full amount returned       |

The platform fee only applies when a campaign successfully completes. If a campaign is refunded, you get 100% back.
